1. Shadow of the Colossus

Shadow of the Colossus is my ultimate all time favorite game ever. Its combination of beautiful scenery,amazing story, stunning music, and fun gameplay has made it stand the test of time, not showing any of in the slightest. The game, for me and many others, has a large sense of awe-making nostalgia that over takes the player the moment the first colossus stomps into view. With its up-coming remake its safe to say the game has and will always hold a special place in the hearts of many gamers. And it will always have a place at the top in my eyes.
